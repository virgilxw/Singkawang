Welcome to Sinkawang

the City of a Thousand Temples

Singkawang is a city of 200,000 people located in Kalimantan, Indonesia. The city is distinct as it is an ethnic enclave. The majority of its population are Hakka, a Han Chinese people who speak their own language.

Nowhere is the cultural identity of the city more visible than in its hundreds of Daoist and Buddhist temples.

This website is designed to facilitate the exploration of the city's many temples.

The data was obtained by Dr Emily Hertzman through on-the-ground field work.
